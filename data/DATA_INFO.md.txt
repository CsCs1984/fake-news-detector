# 📂 Dataset Information

This project uses the **ISOT Fake News Dataset**.  

Since the dataset is large, it is **NOT included in this repository**.  

---

## 📥 How to Download

1️⃣ Go to [Kaggle Dataset Link](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/csmalarkodi/isot-fake-news-dataset)  
2️⃣ Download the ZIP file  
3️⃣ Extract it and you will find two files:
- `Fake.csv` → Fake news articles  
- `True.csv` → Real news articles
